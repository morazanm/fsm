procedure

(ctm-run m w i) → list

  m : ctm
  w : tmtape
  i : natnum
Runs the given machine on the given tape with the head starting at position i (which must be a valid) index into w (without exceeding the length of w). A list containing the state the machine halts in, the position of the head, and the tape is returned.
